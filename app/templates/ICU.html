Ventilator

Ventilators help patients breathe by providing mechanical ventilation. Main components include the control system, oxygen supply, and humidifier.

Patient Monitor

Patient monitors track vital signs such as heart rate, oxygen saturation, and blood pressure. Key components include sensors, display screen, and alarms.

Infusion Pump

Infusion pumps deliver fluids, medications, or nutrients to patients in controlled amounts. The main parts are the pump mechanism, control panel, and tubing system.
