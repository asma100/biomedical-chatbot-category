Hematology Analyzer

Hematology analyzers are used to count and analyze blood cells. The primary components include the sample handler, laser detector, and data processing system.

Chemistry Analyzer

Chemistry analyzers are used to analyze body fluids such as blood or urine. Key parts include the reagent system, photometer, and sample processor.

PCR Machine

PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) machines amplify DNA samples to detect genetic material. Important components are the thermal cycler, control panel, and sample wells.
