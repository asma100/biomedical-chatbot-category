Electrosurgical Unit

Electrosurgical units are used for cutting tissue or cauterizing blood vessels during surgery. Key parts include the generator, handpiece, and grounding plate.

Anesthesia Machine

Anesthesia machines deliver gases and inhalational agents to keep patients unconscious during surgery. Main components include the gas delivery system, vaporizers, and breathing circuit.

Surgical Lights

Surgical lights provide optimal illumination during surgery. Important parts include the light head, intensity controls, and positioning arms.
