MRI

MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) uses powerful magnets and radio waves to create detailed images of organs and tissues. Key components include the magnet, gradient coils, and RF transmitter.

Watch a video about MRI

CT Scanner

CT scanners use X-rays to create cross-sectional images of the body. Important parts include the X-ray tube, detectors, and gantry.

Watch a video about CT Scanners

X-ray Machine

X-ray machines capture images of the body's internal structure using X-rays. The main components are the X-ray tube, image detector, and control console.
